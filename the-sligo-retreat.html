🗺️ Explore Sligo

Sligo is AMAZING 🤩

Mountains, waterfalls, beaches and ancient history — all basically on your doorstep. Here's what you absolutely can't miss!

🏔️ Mountains & Forests

🥾 Iconic Hike
Knocknarea — Queen Maeve's Trail
Panoramic views from the top. An ancient queen's cairn. One of the best hikes in Ireland — genuinely.
🌲 Forest Walk
Benbulben Forest Walk
Beneath Sligo's famous flat-topped mountain. Magical on a misty morning (and they're pretty common here 😂)
🌊 Lake & Woods
Hazelwood — Lough Gill
Beautiful family walks along the lake that inspired W.B. Yeats. Very peaceful!
🏔️ Hidden Valley
Gleniff Horseshoe
A secret valley ringed by mountains. You'll feel like you've stumbled onto a movie set.

💦 Waterfalls

🚶 Easy Walk
Glencar Waterfall
Short walk, absolutely stunning. Great for all ages!
⚡ Wow Factor
Devil's Chimney
Ireland's tallest waterfall. Only flows after rain — but when it does, WOW. Time it right! 🌧️

🏖️ Beaches

🏄 Surf Beach
Strandhill
Wild and windswept. Brilliant for surfing or just walking the dunes at sunset 🌅
👨‍👩‍👧 Family Friendly
Rosses Point
Calmer water, gorgeous coastal walk. Benbulben views from the shoreline 😍
🌊 Drama!
Mullaghmore Head
Cliffs, crashing Atlantic waves. One of the most dramatic spots in all of Ireland.
🤫 Secret Spot
Aughris Head Loop
Sligo's best-kept secret coastal walk. Barely any tourists. Keep it between us 😉

🏛️ History & Culture

⏳ 5,000 Years Old!
Carrowmore Megalithic Cemetery
One of Ireland's largest ancient tomb complexes. Mind-blowing that it even exists tbh.
🏰 Town Centre
Sligo Abbey
A gorgeous 13th-century ruin right in the heart of Sligo town. Great photos!
